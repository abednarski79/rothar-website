What does Rothar do ?

Rothar will help you to find your stolen bicycle.

How do I use Rothar ?

To use Rothar you need to:
  • install Rothar app on your phone
  • buy a tag and attach it to your bike
If your bicycle gets stolen, open the Rothar app and flag your bike as stolen. Everyone in the Rothar network (with Rothar app installed) will start instantly searching for your bicycle. If they find it you will be notified with place and location.

Attaching your tags

Put your tag in a hard-to-find spot on your bike. For example, under the seat, behind a light.
Remember, if the tag comes off - you won't be able to track your bike.

Who came up with this great idea ?

Rothar is the brainchild of Adam Bednarski, a software engineer without a bike (it got stolen). Adam figured there must be a way of using technology to find stolen bicycles... and so he developed Rothar.

Is this really a problem ?

Ooooh yes. In 2003, there were 3,000 bikes stolen in Ireland. By 2012, this had jumped to nearly 5,500! Of these, around 10% get recovered.
If you've had your bike stolen, be sure to look on the Garda photostream to see if it's been recovered.

What does the word "Rothar" mean ?

It means "bike" in the Irish language. Rothar was developed in Ireland.

Is "Rothar" another GPS tracking device ?

No, "Rothar" is using Bluetooth technology which allows for using very cheap tags which can run about 1 year without replacing battery and cost only few Euro.
