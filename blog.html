How it all started

4/13/2015

0 Comments

 
As most of things it was pretty simple story, hard to believe and trivial.
My wife's bike was stolen.
Being developer and curious person make me thinking how to create application allowing for recover of my bike, initially I was thinking about RFID but this has it's own limitation in terms of range and need of receiver antenna.
After few months new phone came to market and thy all got Bluetooth capability also new small and inexpensive tags started to be available - as result of connecting the dots decided to create the Rothar app. And here I'm today.
